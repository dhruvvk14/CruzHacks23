About Waste Identifier

What is the project, and why did we choose it?

 This project was made for CruzHacks 23, for the sustainability track.
When we came to CruzHacks, lots of trash was left after meals, just staying there.
We then cleaned the trash, but when emptying the trash into the bins, we were astonished.
In the compost bin, we saw empty plates, and then it hit us.
People either didn't know how to sort their trash, or didn't care about sustainability.
If we can't do it, can we really expect the next generationt to do it?
This was our motivation for this!
